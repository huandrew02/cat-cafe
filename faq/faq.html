    What is a cat cafe?

    A cat cafe is a unique establishment where customers can enjoy beverages and snacks while spending quality time with resident cats. It provides a cozy and relaxing environment for cat lovers to interact with furry friends.

    What are the hours of operation for the cat cafe?

    Our hours of operation are as follows:
    MON: 10am - 5pm
    TUES: 10am - 5pm
    THURS: 10am - 5pm
    FRI: 9am - 6pm
    SAT: 11am - 5pm
    SUN: Closed
    WED: Closed

    Are there specific time slots for certain activities and age groups?

    Yes, some activities and age groups are restricted to specific time slots. Please review our options before booking to ensure a seamless and enjoyable experience.

    Can I visit the cat cafe during private hours?

    Yes, we offer private visits for both regular and private reservations. Follow the same guidelines, and enjoy 50 minutes in the company of our adoptable cats.
