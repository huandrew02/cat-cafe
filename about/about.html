    • Our mission

    Serving our community since 2015

    Kat Kafe is a cozy haven in the heart of Queens, where volunteers work tirelessly to provide a home for cats in need and promote responsible pet ownership. As the only non-profit cat cafe in the area, we are dedicated to reducing feline homelessness through adoption, education, and outreach programs. With each cup of coffee or pastry purchased, patrons directly contribute to our mission of giving cats a second chance at a loving home. Step inside and meet our playful resident cats, each with their own unique personality waiting to steal your heart.
